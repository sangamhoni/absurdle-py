Absurdle

Wordle's evil twin

The game doesn't pick one secret word. It keeps as many words as possible in play and gives you feedback that could fit any of them until you narrow it down to one.

How to play

  • G Green = correct letter, correct position.
  • Y Yellow = letter in the word, wrong position.
  • W Gray = letter not in the word.

Guesses are unlimited.
